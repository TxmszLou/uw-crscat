KOREAN 134 First-Year Intensive Korean (15)
Covers the fundamentals of the modern Korean language. Includes Hangul, oral communication skills, and basic grammar. Presumes no initial knowledge of Korean. Equivalent to KOREAN 101, KOREAN 102, and KOREAN 103. Cannot be taken for credit in combination with KOREAN 101, KOREAN 102, and KOREAN 103 or KOREAN 135. Students with prior background must take placement test. Offered: S.
View course details in MyPlan: KOREAN 134

KOREAN 135 Accelerated Elementary Korean I (5)
Improves reading for academic purposes. Designed for students with basic speaking ability; those with substantial prior exposure to the language such as growing up speaking Korean at home or having attended a weekend school. First in a sequence of three. Prerequisite: score of 45-65 on the KR100A placement test. Offered: A.
View course details in MyPlan: KOREAN 135
