FINN 150 Intensive First-Year Finnish (15)
Fundamentals of oral and written Finnish. Intensive practice in speaking, reading, and writing. Interactive classroom, computer-assisted learning, and language and reading laboratories. Emphasis on contemporary Finnish culture and society. If Finnish is the student's language of admission, only 10 credits count towards graduation.
