UKR 320 Introduction to Ukrainian Literature and Culture (5) VLPA
Provides an overview of Ukrainian culture: literature, film, music, theatre, art, and architecture, as well as an introduction to Ukrainian cultural life. Taught in English.
View course details in MyPlan: UKR 320

UKR 401 First-Year Ukrainian (5)
Introduction to spoken and written Ukrainian. First in a sequence of three.
View course details in MyPlan: UKR 401

UKR 402 First-Year Ukrainian (5)
Introduction to spoken and written Ukrainian. Second in a sequence of three. Prerequisite: UKR 401, which may be taken concurrently.
View course details in MyPlan: UKR 402

UKR 403 First-Year Ukrainian (5)
Introduction to spoken and written Ukrainian. Third in a sequence of three. Prerequisite: UKR 402, which may be taken concurrently.
View course details in MyPlan: UKR 403

UKR 420 Literature, Film, and Culture of Ukraine (5) VLPA
Representative prose works by leading Ukrainian authors. Shows originality of Ukrainian literature through acquaintance with the peculiar historical and political situation of Ukraine. Offered in English.
