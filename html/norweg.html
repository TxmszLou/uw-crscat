NORW 150 Intensive First-Year Norwegian (15)
Fundamentals of oral and written Norwegian. Intensive practice in speaking, reading, and writing. Interactive classroom, computer-assisted learning, language and reading laboratories. Emphasis on contemporary Norwegian culture and society. If Norwegian is the student's language of admission, only 10 credits count towards graduation.
