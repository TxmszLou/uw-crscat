EGYPT 101 Hieroglyphic Egyptian I (5)
Provides an introduction to hieroglyphic Egyptian as written during the Middle Kingdom (circa 2040-1782 BCE). Students learn to read and write basic hieroglyphics so they can read a complete Egyptian text. No prior knowledge required.
