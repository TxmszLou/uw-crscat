KAZAKH 101 Elementary Kazakh (5)
Emphasizes on the four basic language skills: listening, speaking, reading, and writing in Kazakh. Focuses on exchanging real-life information in the context of the native-speaking environment and understanding Kazakh language through the cultural contexts. Cannot be taken for credit if KAZAKH 105 taken. First in a sequence of three.

KAZAKH 105 Intensive Elementary Kazakh (15)
Intensive study of grammar, with oral and written drill and reading of simple texts in Kazak. Covers first-year Kazak. Cannot be taken for credit if KAZAKH 101, KAZAKH 102, KAZAKH 103 taken.
View course details in MyPlan: KAZAKH 105

KAZAKH 201 Intermediate Kazakh (5) VLPA
Designed to strengthen and develop further skills in listening, speaking, reading and writing and to deepen an understanding of the cultural context of Kazakh. Emphasis on the expansion and refinement of linguistic and communicative skills learned in elementary Kazakh. Prerequisite: either KAZAKH 103 or KAZAKH 105.

KAZAKH 205 Intensive Intermediate Kazakh (15) VLPA
Allows students to complete second-year Kazak in one quarter. Reading of selected texts in modern literary Kazak, with emphasis on grammar, syntax, and oral practice. Prerequisite: either KAZAKH 103 or KAZAKH 105.
