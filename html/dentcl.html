DENTCL 603 Patient Advocacy - Third Year (2) J. EVANS
Teaches third year dental students the practice of comprehensive patient care. Students required to monitor the care of 30 to 50 patients and make recommendations with regard to treatment sequence and recall strategies. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DENTCL 603

DENTCL 631 Prosthodontics Clerkship (9) Y. CHEN
Teaches students to understand prosthodontics needs of patients and to design and deliver treatment. The clinical experience is supported with the didactic training during the clerkship. At the end of the clerkship, student dentists are prepared to practice in the Comprehensive Care 4th year. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DENTCL 631

DENTCL 632 Periodontics Clerkship (9) C. HERRON
Covers general principles in clinical periodontics both in class and in the clinical clerkship setting. Students develop an overall understanding of the scope of periodontal treatment and clinical decision-making by active participation, integrating periodontal therapy in a comprehensive plan of care. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DENTCL 632

DENTCL 633 Endodontics Clerkship (9) N. FLAKE
Prepares dental students to provide endodontic therapy within the scope of general dentistry. In the seminar component, students expand their didactic knowledge in preparation for clinical experiences. In the clinical component, student treat patients, including diagnosis, treatment planning, and endodontic therapy of minimal-moderate difficulty level. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DENTCL 633

DENTCL 634 Operative Dentistry Clerkship (9) D. Chan
Operative dentistry plays a key role in disease control and the integration of comprehensive care of our patients. Emphasizes preventive therapies for caries. Students diagnose and treat conditions of the human dentition that fall within the scope of operative dentistry and utilizes the patient assessment and treatment planning process in order to deliver appropriate preventive and restorative procedures. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DENTCL 634

DENTCL 635 Diagnosis and Treatment Planning Clerkship (9) J. APS
Familiarizes students with the procedure of a comprehensive, thorough, oral and head and neck examination, taking medical and dental history and ethically justified decision making in patient individual treatment planning. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DENTCL 635

DENTCL 636 Oral Medicine Clerkship (9) K. MANOOKIN
Provides clinical experience and develops competency in the diagnosis and management of acute and chronic orofacial pain, diseases of the oral mucosa, salivary dysfunction, oral manifestations of systemic diseases, dental management of patients with developmental or other disabilities, and taking interpretation of radiographs. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DENTCL 636

DENTCL 637 Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery Clerkship (9) J. EVANS
Introduces students to the specialty of oral and maxillofacial surgery. An equally important goal is to supply students with the knowledge base and clinical skills necessary to practice general dentistry in our communities. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DENTCL 637

DENTCL 638 Pediatric Dentistry and Orthodontic Clerkship (9) B. BAYIRLI, M. GARCIA
Didactic and clinical educational experiences in comprehensive care of pediatric dental patients in the areas of prevention, diagnosis, treatment planning, and restorative dentistry. In addition, recognition, analysis and treatment planning of malocclusions as well as introduction to limited tooth movement. Offered: AWSpS.
